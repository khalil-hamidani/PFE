If there is an immediate threat to life or risk of harm, call your local police services

If you believe a cyber incident is an imminent threat to life or of a criminal nature, please contact your local police services. We encourage all victims to report cybercrime activities to law enforcement.

Submitting a report using this service

Reporting a cyber incident consists of five sections and takes approximately 10 minutes.

  1. Complainant's Information
  2. Company Informations
  3. Company or Person Complained Against
  4. description of the incident
  5. Evidences

Before you report:

Please make sure you have the following details ready before you start:

  • The Algerian Business Number (ABN)
  • Details of what happened, including dates and any financial transactions made
  • Whether the victim is willing to make a formal statement

Your report is important:

By reporting, you will be helping to stop other people from falling victim to the same issue. Your report will also assist the Algerian Cyber Security Center and law enforcement agencies to disrupt cyber-crime operations and make Algeria the safest place to connect online.

Sharing informative reports is crucial for us to comprehend the threat landscape and enhance the cyber intelligence capabilities. This subsequently improves our ability to help protect other Algerian organisations.
