Report Personal cyber issue

If there is an immediate threat to life or risk of harm call your local police services

If you believe a cyber incident is an imminent threat to life or of a criminal nature, please contact your local police services. We encourage all victims to report cybercrime activities to law enforcement.

Submitting a report using this service

Reporting a cyber incident consists of four sections and takes approximately 10 minutes.

  1. Complainant's Information
  2. Company or Person Complained Against
  3. description of the incident
  4. Evidences

Before you report:

Please make sure you have the following details ready before you start:

  • Contact information for you
  • Contact information for your organisation or the organisation you are reporting on behalf of
  • Any details of how the cyber incident started and how the organisation was affected

Your report is important:

By reporting, you will be helping to stop other people from falling victim to the same issue. Your report will also assist the Algerian Cyber Security Centre and law enforcement agencies to disrupt cyber-crime operations and make Algeria the safest place to connect online.
